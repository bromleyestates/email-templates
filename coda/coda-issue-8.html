A Conversation with Darren Bader,
Artist & Calder Prize Laureate

Each issue, The Coda introduces you to interesting individuals who inspire us. This time, we sat down to talk with visionary Darren Bader, a contemporary artist on the rise and the recent recipient of the Calder Foundation’s most coveted and greatly revered Calder Prize. For a young artist to have had over ten solo exhibitions, including one at MOMA PS1, a handful of published books (one co-authored/envisaged by Urs Fischer), and countless group shows since his ascent into art began over a decade ago—it is the flourishing path of one, Darren Bader…

Head over to our Tumblr for his full interview and photos.
