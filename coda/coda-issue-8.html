A Conversation with Vogue writers Thessaly La Force and Katherine Bernard

Each issue, The Coda introduces you to interesting individuals who inspire us. This time, we sat down to talk with Vogue writers Thessaly La Force and Katherine Bernard. Not only are they colleagues, they are also dear friends; two women who personify and redefine intelligence, wit, and beauty.

Thessaly is the culture editor of Vogue.com. She was formerly an editor at The Paris Review and a web producer at The New Yorker, and is the co-editor and co-founder of Girl Crush Zine. Katherine is a writer for renowned publications and sites including The Paris Review Daily, The Wall Street Journal, and Dazed & Confused. She is also the founder and editor of Henry Review, a fantastic Webby-nominated digital reading series.
