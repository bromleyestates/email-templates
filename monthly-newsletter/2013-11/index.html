White Light Festival opens October 24

The White Light Festival, our annual celebration of the transcendent power of music, kicks off October 24 with a free opening night concert by The Campbell Brothers. Don't miss this season's powerful performances, including Le Concert d’Astrée's Metamorphosis, Michel van der Aa's Up-close, and much more.

View the Calendar

White Light Festival is sponsored by Time Warner Inc.

Please note, the ticketing system used by Lincoln Center will be upgraded starting at 9:00 pm on Tuesday, October 1. Ticketing for Great Performers and White Light Festival will be unavailable from that time until approximately noon on Wednesday, October 2.
