Opera, film, ballet, and classical-music opening nights this month

Lincoln Center comes alive this September with spectacular opening nights. Don’t miss New York City Ballet’s Swan Lake, Eugene Onegin at The Metropolitan Opera, the New York Philharmonic’s opening gala with Yo‑Yo Ma, and the 51st New York Film Festival.
